Why You Should Join the TheoreX Pilot Program?

TheoreX brings up the world's first E-Learning Portal, where one will not only learn but also can showcase his or her expertise,
he or she can connect with other professionals and also can choose the right job for himself or herself choosing the right
company where he or she can suit perfectly according to his or her culture, experience, knowledge and expertise.



Responsibilities

Working as a Catalyst

A TheoreX pilot is expected to be of help to the company in catalysing business communications between the company and various institutional bodies.

Meet initiator

A TheoreX pilot takes up the primary initiative to organise an in-person interaction between institutes and TheoreX team

Event manager for TheoreX

TheoreX pilot should report to the company, informing minute and vast possibilities for events in interest of TheoreX.

Social Media Influence

An appointed TheoreX pilot is expected to be an active entity in social media, which should help the company to create public awareness about Theorex

Offline Marketing Weapon

Theorex requires the pilot to bear word of the mouth and be the most natural agent for marketing for the company. An impactful offline marketing strategy will be highly appreciated.

Recruiting Campus Champs

A pilot should appoint campus representatives for the various institutes it associates the company with. These campus champs act as information gatherers and mqrketing tributaries.

Collaboration with Rockstar Moderator

A TheoreX pilot is supposed to report to his/her respective moderator. Moderators will be TheoreX employees,who shall be majorly responsible to groom the pilots in their job associated with TheoreX along with providing insight to the pilots about their job, trade, interest and profession.

Submitting Monthly Reports

A TheoreX pilot is expected to submit a monthly report to TheoreX through respective moderators. Transparency of reports and viability of analysis submitted by the pilots will reflect upon his/her performance recount which will be monitored by the company.
